Our Legacy

A Heritage Built on
Uncompromising Standards

Founded in 1946, RH Freeman began with a simple yet profound promise: to deliver metalwork of the highest standard. In an era where every cut and weld was a testament to the maker's hand, we forged a reputation for reliability that has stood the test of time.

Over seven decades later, while our tools have evolved from manual lathes to servo electric-powered CNC machinery, our philosophy remains unchanged. In 1991 we became the first company of our type in New Zealand to be certified to ISO 9000 — a commitment to quality that continues to define everything we do.

Under new ownership since October 2025, RH Freeman enters its next chapter with the same founding commitment to precision, quality, and craftsmanship that has defined us for nearly eighty years.
